=== THERAPEUTIC CONTENT (8-10 minutes) ===

Receive only what serves you. Your subconscious mind will accept what is true for you and let the rest go.

[PAUSE 3 SECONDS]

--- Perfection Is Impossible ---

Perfection is impossible. Nothing you create will be flawless. Demanding the impossible guarantees frustration.

[PAUSE 5 SECONDS]

--- What "Good Enough" Means ---

"Good enough" means meeting requirements without unnecessary polish. An email that communicates is good enough. A presentation that conveys your points is good enough.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

Most situations call for "good enough," not perfect. Wisdom is knowing the difference.

[PAUSE 5 SECONDS]

--- Done Is Better Than Perfect ---

A completed project, even imperfect, has value. An uncompleted project, endlessly polished, has none.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

Imperfect action beats perfect inaction. A good enough solution today beats a perfect solution that never arrives.

[PAUSE 5 SECONDS]

--- The 80/20 Principle ---

80 percent of results come from 20 percent of effort. The last 20 percent often requires 80 percent more effort.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

Wisdom recognizes when you have reached "good enough" and moves on.

[PAUSE 5 SECONDS]

--- Practicing "Good Enough" ---

Before starting: "What is good enough for this?"
While working: "Am I past good enough?"
When finished: "This is good enough. Done is better than perfect."

[PAUSE 5 SECONDS]

--- Anchor Reinforcement ---

Bring thumb and pointer finger together, reinforcing your anchor.

[PAUSE 3 SECONDS]

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

Release the gesture.
