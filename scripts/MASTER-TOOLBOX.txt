HOW TO USE ANCHORS:
1. Make the gesture
2. Hold for 3-5 seconds while breathing deeply
3. Recall the associated affirmation or state
4. Release and proceed with clarity

Anchors grow stronger with each use. Use them consistently.

===============================================================================
                        SERIES 1: ANXIETY
===============================================================================
Anchor: Thumb + finger pressed together
Affirmation: "I am calm, centered, and at peace"

Top 3 Tools:
1. BOX BREATHING: Inhale 4 counts, hold 4, exhale 4, hold 4. Repeat 4+ times.
2. GROUNDING (5-4-3-2-1): Name 5 things you see, 4 hear, 3 feel, 2 smell, 1 taste.
3. THOUGHT DEFUSION: Notice anxious thoughts as "I'm having the thought that..."

See: /scripts/anxiety/toolbox-*.txt (for individual toolbox)

===============================================================================
                        SERIES 2: SELF-CONFIDENCE
===============================================================================
Anchor: Thumb + ring finger pressed together
Affirmation: "I am capable, worthy, and confident"

Top 3 Tools:
1. POWER POSE: Stand tall, shoulders back, chest open for 2 minutes before challenges.
2. EVIDENCE LOG: Write daily evidence that contradicts limiting beliefs.
3. PAST SUCCESSES: List 5 past achievements to recall before challenging situations.

See: /scripts/self-confidence/toolbox-*.txt

===============================================================================
                        SERIES 3: PHONE ADDICTION
===============================================================================
Anchor: Thumb + pinky finger pressed together
Affirmation: "I am present and in control of my attention"

Top 3 Tools:
1. PAUSE PROTOCOL: Before picking up phone, ask "Why?" Wait 5 seconds.
2. PHONE ZONES: Designate no-phone times/places (meals, bedroom, etc.)
3. APP LIMITS: Set daily limits on high-usage apps.

See: /scripts/phone-addiction/toolbox-*.txt

===============================================================================
                        SERIES 4: OVERTHINKING
===============================================================================
Anchor: Thumb + middle finger pressed together
Affirmation: "My mind is clear, calm, and focused"

Top 3 Tools:
1. THOUGHT BOX: Visualize placing worries in a box to address at scheduled time.
2. DECISION DEADLINE: Give yourself a time limit, then decide and move on.
3. WORRY WINDOW: Schedule 15 minutes daily for worrying; contain it to that time.

See: /scripts/overthinking/toolbox-*.txt

===============================================================================
                        SERIES 5: NAIL BITING
===============================================================================
Anchor: Thumb + first finger pressed together
Affirmation: "My hands are calm and my nails are healthy"

Top 3 Tools:
1. WRIST TAP: When urge arises, tap wrist 3 times to interrupt pattern.
2. COMPETING RESPONSE: Make gentle fist for 1-2 minutes when urge hits.
3. URGE SURFING: Observe urge without acting; it peaks and passes in 90 seconds.

See: /scripts/nail-biting/toolbox-*.txt

===============================================================================
                    SERIES 6: PORNOGRAPHY/MASTURBATION
===============================================================================
Anchor: Thumb + second finger pressed together
Affirmation: "I am free and in control of my choices"

Top 3 Tools:
1. HALT CHECK: When tempted, ask: Am I Hungry, Angry, Lonely, or Tired?
2. STOP TECHNIQUE: Stop, Think (of consequences), Observe (feeling), Proceed wisely.
3. PLAY TAPE FORWARD: Imagine how you'll feel 5 minutes, 1 hour after acting out.

See: /scripts/pornography-masturbation/toolbox-*.txt

===============================================================================
                      SERIES 7: PRESENT WITH FAMILY
===============================================================================
Anchor: Hand over heart
Affirmation: "I am fully present with those I love"

Top 3 Tools:
1. TRANSITION RITUAL: Before entering home, take 3 breaths, set intention to be present.
2. DEVICE DOWN: Phone away during family meals and quality time.
3. EYE CONTACT MOMENTS: Make deliberate eye contact when family speaks to you.

See: /scripts/present-with-family/toolbox-*.txt

===============================================================================
                       SERIES 8: GUILT AND SHAME
===============================================================================
Anchor: Palm on chest (self-compassion gesture)
Affirmation: "I am worthy of compassion. My worth is not defined by my mistakes."

Top 3 Tools:
1. GUILT VS SHAME: Guilt = "I did something bad" (healthy). Shame = "I am bad" (toxic).
2. SELF-COMPASSION BREAK: Touch chest, acknowledge suffering, offer kindness.
3. SHAME STORY REWRITE: Challenge shame narratives with more complete truth.

See: /scripts/guilt-and-shame/toolbox-*.txt

===============================================================================
                      SERIES 9: DEVELOPING PATIENCE
===============================================================================
Anchor: Interlaced fingers
Affirmation: "I have all the time I need. Patience flows through me."

Top 3 Tools:
1. THREE-BREATH PAUSE: When impatient, take 3 slow breaths before responding.
2. REFRAME WAITING: Ask "How can I use this time?" vs. "Why is this taking so long?"
3. PROGRESS FOCUS: When goal is distant, celebrate small steps forward.

See: /scripts/developing-patience/toolbox-*.txt

===============================================================================
                     SERIES 10: OVERCOMING MISOPHONIA
===============================================================================
Anchor: Thumb pressing into center of opposite palm
Affirmation: "Sounds cannot harm me. I am safe and in control."

Top 3 Tools:
1. NOTICE-BREATHE-ANCHOR: When triggered, notice, breathe deeply, press anchor.
2. DISTANCE TECHNIQUE: Visualize trigger sounds getting farther away.
3. REFRAME: "This is just a sound. It cannot hurt me. I choose my response."

See: /scripts/overcoming-misophonia/toolbox-*.txt

===============================================================================
                   SERIES 11: OVERCOMING PERFECTIONISM
===============================================================================
Anchor: OK gesture (thumb and pointer finger in circle)
Affirmation: "Done is better than perfect. I embrace good enough."

Top 3 Tools:
1. 80/20 STANDARD: Ask "What's 80% good enough?" rather than aiming for 100%.
2. FIRST DRAFT FREEDOM: Give yourself permission to create imperfect first versions.
3. SELF-COMPASSION: Talk to yourself as you would a struggling friend.

See: /scripts/overcoming-perfectionism/toolbox-*.txt

===============================================================================
                      SERIES 12: OVERCOMING LUST
===============================================================================
Anchor: Touching center of forehead
Affirmation: "I am master of my thoughts. I guard my heart."

Top 3 Tools:
1. NOTICE-RELEASE-REDIRECT: Notice thought, release without fighting, redirect attention.
2. BOUNCE TECHNIQUE: When eyes land on trigger, immediately look elsewhere. No second look.
3. ENERGY REDIRECT: Channel sexual energy into physical, creative, or spiritual pursuits.

See: /scripts/overcoming-lust/toolbox-*.txt

===============================================================================
                    SERIES 13: MOTIVATION AND DRIVE
===============================================================================
Anchor: Make a fist
Affirmation: "I am a person of purpose and drive. Action creates motivation."

Top 3 Tools:
1. TWO-MINUTE RULE: Commit to just 2 minutes of any task. Starting creates momentum.
2. WHY STATEMENT: Write and review your purpose: "I exist to ___ so that ___."
3. FIVE-SECOND RULE: Count 5-4-3-2-1 and MOVE before your brain makes excuses.

See: /scripts/motivation-and-drive/toolbox-*.txt

===============================================================================
                           DAILY PRACTICES
===============================================================================

MORNING (5-10 minutes):
- Set intention for the day
- Activate relevant anchor(s) for today's challenges
- Review your "why" or purpose statement
- Gratitude practice (3 things)

THROUGHOUT THE DAY:
- Use anchors as needed
- Apply relevant tools from active series
- Check in with body (hunger, energy, tension)
- Pause before reacting (3 breaths)

EVENING (5-10 minutes):
- Review day: What went well? What could improve?
- Self-compassion for any struggles
- Wind-down routine for sleep
- Gratitude practice (3 things)

WEEKLY:
- Full review of current focus series
- Check progress on habits
- Adjust practices as needed

MONTHLY:
- Listen to reinforcement session from focus series
- Review toolbox for refresh
- Celebrate progress
