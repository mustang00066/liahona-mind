=== THERAPEUTIC CONTENT (8-10 minutes) ===

--- Why Sleep Matters ---

Sleep cleans your brain, consolidates memory, regulates hormones, and restores willpower. Every system depends on it.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

Most adults need seven to nine hours. Not five or six. Seven to nine.

[PAUSE 5 SECONDS]

--- What Blocks Sleep ---

Blue light from screens suppresses melatonin. Caffeine after noon lingers into night. Alcohol disrupts sleep quality. Irregular schedules confuse your rhythm.

[PAUSE 5 SECONDS]

--- Sleep Hygiene ---

Consistent schedule: Same bedtime and wake time daily.

[PAUSE 3 SECONDS]

Cool, dark, quiet room: 65-68 degrees, blackout curtains, minimal noise.

[PAUSE 3 SECONDS]

Screen curfew: No screens one hour before bed.

[PAUSE 3 SECONDS]

Wind-down routine: Calming activities that signal sleep is coming.

[PAUSE 5 SECONDS]

--- Falling Asleep Techniques ---

The 4-7-8 breath: Inhale four counts, hold seven, exhale eight. Activates relaxation.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

Body scan: Progressively relax each body part from toes to head.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

If you cannot sleep after twenty minutes, get up and do something calming, then return when drowsy.

[PAUSE 5 SECONDS]

[PAUSE 3 SECONDS]
