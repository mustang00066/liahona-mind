=== THERAPEUTIC CONTENT (8-10 minutes) ===

Receive only what serves you. Your subconscious mind will accept what is true for you and let the rest go.

[PAUSE 3 SECONDS]

--- Why Movement Matters ---

Movement improves cardiovascular health, strengthens bones and joints, regulates metabolism, and releases mood-improving endorphins.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

Sedentary living reverses all of this. The body was designed to move—when it does not, it declines.

[PAUSE 5 SECONDS]

--- Movement as Celebration ---

Movement is not punishment. It is celebration—using your body for what it was designed. You exercise because you love your body, not because you hate it.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

The best exercise is the one you will actually do. Find movement you enjoy enough to keep doing.

[PAUSE 5 SECONDS]

--- Components of Fitness ---

Cardiovascular: walking, running, cycling—elevates heart rate.

[PAUSE 3 SECONDS]

Strength: weights, bodyweight exercises—maintains muscle and bone.

[PAUSE 3 SECONDS]

Flexibility: stretching, yoga—keeps joints healthy.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

Incorporate each type across your week.

[PAUSE 5 SECONDS]

--- Starting and Sustaining ---

Start small. Even ten minutes of walking daily is infinitely better than nothing. Build gradually.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

Consistency over intensity. A moderate practice you maintain beats an intense practice you abandon.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

Schedule exercise like an appointment. Weave movement throughout your day.

[PAUSE 5 SECONDS]

--- Anchor Reinforcement ---

Place your hand on your belly, reinforcing your anchor.

[PAUSE 3 SECONDS]

This anchor carries love of movement. Say internally: "I love to move my body. Movement is celebration."

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

Release your hand.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

=== POST-HYPNOTIC SUGGESTIONS (1 minute) ===

You will see movement as celebration, not punishment.

[PAUSE 3 SECONDS]

You will find movement you enjoy and build it into your life. Consistency over intensity.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

Welcome back, Austin. This week, find movement you enjoy and start—even if just for ten minutes. Let movement become a gift you give yourself.
