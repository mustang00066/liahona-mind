=== THERAPEUTIC CONTENT (8-10 minutes) ===

--- Understanding Mindful Use ---

Austin, mindful technology use has three components. First, intention: knowing what you want to do before you pick up the phone. Second, awareness: staying conscious while using it. Third, completion: putting it down when your purpose is fulfilled.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

The opposite is automatic use. Reaching without thinking. Opening apps from habit. Scrolling without purpose. Losing time without intention.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

--- The PAUSE Protocol ---

Here is a practical protocol for mindful phone use. PAUSE.

[PAUSE 3 SECONDS]

P is for Purpose. Before picking up your phone, ask: What am I going to do with this? Have a clear answer before touching it.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

A is for Awareness. While using the phone, stay conscious. Notice if you drift from your stated purpose. When you catch yourself drifting, return to intention.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

U is for Understanding your limits. Before you begin, decide when you will stop. Know your endpoint before you start.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

S is for Stopping. When your purpose is fulfilled, stop. Resist "just one more thing." You completed your intention. That is enough.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

E is for Evaluation. After putting the phone down, briefly notice how you feel. Did the interaction serve you? This reflection strengthens future mindfulness.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

--- Practicing the Protocol ---

Imagine you need to check your calendar. Before picking up the phone, you state your purpose: "I am going to look at my calendar for tomorrow."

[PAUSE 3 SECONDS]

You pick up the phone with clear intention. Navigate directly to the calendar. See tomorrow's appointments. Your purpose is fulfilled.

[PAUSE 3 SECONDS]

The urge to check email or social media may arise. But you completed your intention. You stop. You put the phone down. Clean. Satisfying.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

--- Managing the Drift ---

Even with good intentions, you will sometimes drift. This is normal. The key is what you do when you notice.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

The mindful response is not self-criticism. It is gentle noticing. "I drifted from my intention." Then choose: return to your purpose or consciously decide to engage with the new thing.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

With practice, you catch the drift earlier. Eventually you notice the pull before you follow it. This is progress.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

--- Scripture Principle ---

The scriptures counsel doing all things in wisdom and order. Mindful phone use is wisdom and order. Not prohibition, but intentional engagement.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]
