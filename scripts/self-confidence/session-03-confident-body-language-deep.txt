Research shows that body posture affects emotions. When you stand tall, you feel confident. When you slump, you feel defeated. The body leads, the mind follows.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

Confident body language has specific elements. Head held high, chin parallel to the ground. Shoulders back and down. Chest open. Spine straight but not rigid.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

Confident people take up space. They do not shrink themselves. They stand with feet hip-width apart. They gesture freely. They do not hide.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

From now on, you will check your posture regularly. Before important moments. When entering rooms. When speaking to others. You will reset to confident posture.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

This will become automatic. Your default posture will become confident. Your body will express the confidence you are building inside.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

Your body language affects how others perceive you too. Confident posture commands respect. It says: I know my worth. Others respond to this.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]
