SELF CONFIDENCE - SESSION 4: Speaking with Confidence (QUICK VERSION)
Duration: ~28 minutes
Voice: Male, British accent, calm and affirming

[BEGIN SCRIPT]

Welcome back, Austin. Today we focus on your voice. Many feel confident until they speak. You will learn to express yourself with assurance.

[PAUSE 3 SECONDS]

Take a deep breath in... hold... and release, feeling your voice ready.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

Another breath, deeper... breathing into throat and chest... hold... and release.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

One more breath... eyes closing, voice centering.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

Let the golden light empower speech. Throat warming. Opening. Relaxing. Voice preparing.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

Descend now. Ten to one. Ten, nine, eight, seven, six, five, four, three, two, one. Vocal confidence.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

Confident speech has qualities. First, pace. Do not rush. Speak at a measured pace.

[PAUSE 3 SECONDS]

Second, volume. Be audible. Project clearly, not loudly.

[PAUSE 3 SECONDS]

Third, pauses. Do not fear silence. Pauses show comfort.

[PAUSE 3 SECONDS]

Fourth, pitch. Let your voice stay level or drop at the end of statements.

[PAUSE 3 SECONDS]

Fifth, minimize um and uh. Replace with brief pauses.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

Imagine speaking slowly. Voice clear. Pausing between sentences. Voice level at the end. Confident. Certain.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

The scripture promises the Lord will give you words when needed. Trust your voice.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

Before speaking in important situations, take a breath. Center yourself. Speak slowly, clearly, with assurance.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

If nervous, press your worth anchor. Your voice deserves to be heard. Your thoughts have value.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

With practice, confident speech becomes natural. Others listen more closely.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

One... beginning to return.
Two... feeling articulate and clear.
Three... becoming alert.
Four... refreshed and expressive.
Five... eyes open, fully alert. Your voice is confident, Austin. Speak and be heard.

[END SCRIPT]
