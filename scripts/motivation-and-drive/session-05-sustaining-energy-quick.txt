=== THERAPEUTIC CONTENT (8-10 minutes) ===

Receive only what serves you. Your subconscious mind will accept what is true for you and let the rest go.

[PAUSE 3 SECONDS]

--- The Four Sources ---

Physical energy: Sleep, nutrition, movement, hydration. The foundation.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

Emotional energy: Positive emotions give energy. Negative emotions drain it. Cultivate gratitude. Process what needs processing.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

Mental energy: Finite and precious. Reduce decision fatigue. Batch similar tasks. Take breaks.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

Spiritual energy: Purpose-driven people have more stamina. Stay connected to your why. Nurture your spiritual life.

[PAUSE 5 SECONDS]

--- Physical Foundation ---

Protect your sleep—seven to nine hours. Create conditions for quality rest.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

Eat for stable energy—protein, vegetables, healthy fats.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

Move regularly—exercise creates energy despite using it.

[PAUSE 5 SECONDS]

--- Energy Rhythms ---

Energy rises and falls throughout the day. Match important work to high-energy times.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

Respect the rhythms. Do not fight dips with caffeine. Brief rest brings the next wave naturally.

[PAUSE 5 SECONDS]

--- Recovery ---

Daily recovery: Short breaks, time disconnected.

[PAUSE 3 SECONDS]

Weekly recovery: One day for rest and renewal.

[PAUSE 3 SECONDS]

Quarterly recovery: Vacations and retreats for deeper renewal.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

You cannot give what you do not have.

[PAUSE 5 SECONDS]

--- Anchor Reinforcement ---

Make a fist, reinforcing your drive anchor.

[PAUSE 3 SECONDS]

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

Release your fist.
