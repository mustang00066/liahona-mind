=== THERAPEUTIC CONTENT (8-10 minutes) ===

--- Understanding Procrastination ---

Procrastination is not about time management. It is about emotion management. You delay to avoid uncomfortable feelings.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

Fear of failure. Perfectionism. Overwhelm. Boredom. Understanding which emotion is driving you helps you address it directly.

[PAUSE 5 SECONDS]

--- Addressing the Roots ---

If fear of failure: Remember—failure is information, not identity. Imperfect action beats perfect inaction.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

If overwhelm: Break the task down. Identify just the first step. Clarify what done looks like.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

If boredom: Make the task interesting. Pair it with something enjoyable. Find the meaning.

[PAUSE 5 SECONDS]

--- Practical Strategies ---

Time blocking: Schedule specific times for specific tasks.

[PAUSE 3 SECONDS]

The five-second rule: Count down from five, then move. No time for excuses.

[PAUSE 3 SECONDS]

Temptation bundling: Pair procrastinated tasks with things you enjoy.

[PAUSE 3 SECONDS]

Environment design: Remove friction between you and action.

[PAUSE 5 SECONDS]

--- Forgiving Yourself ---

Research shows self-forgiveness reduces future procrastination. Shame makes it worse.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

Acknowledge the delay. Learn from it. Let it go. Start fresh.

[PAUSE 5 SECONDS]

--- Anchor Reinforcement ---

Make a fist, reinforcing your drive anchor.

[PAUSE 3 SECONDS]

This anchor carries power over procrastination. Say internally: "I act despite discomfort. I overcome delay."

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]

Release your fist.

[PAUSE 4 SECONDS]
