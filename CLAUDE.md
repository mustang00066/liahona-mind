## Key Techniques
- Eye fatigue induction (James Braid method - upward gaze until eyes tire)
- Diaphragmatic/belly breathing with vagus nerve activation
- Progressive relaxation: "limp, loose, and heavy" / "like lead weights"
- Reciprocal deepening: "the better you feel, the deeper you go"
- Eye catalepsy: "eyelids stuck together"
- NLP anchoring (thumb + finger combinations)
- Progressive anchor building across sessions
- Future pacing (tomorrow, week, month visualizations)
